Welcome!

This is the best recipe for the signatue Argintinian dish Matahambre.Pronounced Muh taam bray.
The name is derived from a cobination of Matar and Hambre meaning hunger killer. THe dish
varies by region but generaly a thin cut of beef is rolled with vegetables, peppers, ham, hard-boiled
eggs, herbs and spices. Boiled in some regions but i find its best rooasted over an open flame to bring
out the best flavors. Matahambre is best eaten accompanied with chimichurri sauce.
lets start with a list of ingredients you will need for the main dish and the sauce

Ingredients

  • Flank steak
  • Bell peppers
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Cilantro
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt & pepper
  • Olive oil(or oil of your prefrence)

Steps

  1. Butterfly the Flank Steak

    2. Using a sharp knife slice the steak open like a book

  2. Make the Chimichurri sauce

    3. Add the olive oil, cilantro, parlsey, garlic, salt & pepper, and the red pepper flakes
    to a food processor and corursley blend

  3. Boil and peel your eggs

    4. I prefer a softer boil but its up to your preferences

  4. Spread the chimichurri over the inside of the steak
  5. Arrange the eggs, pepepers, and vegetable in three seperate rows across the steak
  6. Roll the steak over the filling across the grain and use twine to tie it so it all stays together
  7. Season the ouside of the steak with salt & pepper
  8. Grill the steak

    9. Grill should be at 400 degrees. Grill each side between 4-5min
    total cooking time should take around 20 min. let rest for 15 min before cutting.
